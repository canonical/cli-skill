Recommendation Compliance Notes

Per DE013 grammar, the strongest naming issue is show-engine, followed by completion bash.

Recommended change only if the team is willing to pay migration cost: add a preferred observational alias such as engine or engine-status in the next minor release while keeping show-engine working; emit a stderr warning; keep that aliasing for at least one release cycle; then remove the old form in the next major release with exit code 2 and a replacement message. For completion bash, the standard conflict is real, but the benefit of renaming is smaller and should only be pursued as part of a broader cleanup.
