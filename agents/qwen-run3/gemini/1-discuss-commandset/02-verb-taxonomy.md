# Verb Taxonomy

| verb | frequency | Canonical standard compliance | notes |
|------|-----------|-------------------------------|-------|
| `use` | 1 | Non-standard | `Canonical CLI Standards` prefers standard terms like `config` or `set`. Using `use-engine` violates the strict noun-modifier patterns. |
| `show` | 1 | Compliant | Standard verb for showing details of one instance. Used correctly as `show-engine`. |
| `get` | 1 | Compliant | Standard for retrieving configuration. |
| `set` | 1 | Compliant | Standard for setting configuration. |
| `chat` | 1 | Non-standard | `chat` is specific domain behavior. Can be analyzed as an interactive `start` or standalone tool. |
