Recommendations

1. Rename webui → open-webui

Standard: DE013 §Grammar — "Commands are verbs"

Cost: Low | Impact: High clarity

Deprecation: Minor version adds open-webui with alias; major version removes webui

2. Clarify prune-cache naming

Standard: DE013 §Grammar — verb must "trigger recall of the object type"

Cost: Medium | Impact: Medium clarity

Option A: Rename to remove-components

Option B: Keep name, improve help text to clarify what "cache" means

3. Add --dry-run to destructive commands

Standard: DE013 §Safety — "dry-run support"

Cost: Low | Impact: High safety

Applies to: use-engine, prune-cache

4. Standardize --format options

Standard: DE013 §Consistency

Cost: Low | Impact: High consistency

Change: All observation commands support table, json, yaml

5. Document server lifecycle in help

Standard: DE013 §Discoverability

Cost: Low | Impact: High discoverability
